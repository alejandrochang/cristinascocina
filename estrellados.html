Huevos Estrellados!

  • Written by Alejandro Chang                                 
  • Food                                 

Huevos Estrellados is perhaps my favorite breakfast item ever. Its delicious, filling and health for any occasion. It also requires little to no ingredients.

Ingredients you will need:

• Tortillas (corn) - * if wanted, but not needed
• Eggs (2 eggs per person)
• Cooking Oil / Butter (whatever you use)
• Ben Franks Sausage or Ham (not needed but makes it extra tasty)
• Tapatio
This cooking process should literally take you 10-15 minutes at most, depending on how many plates you're making.

If you're using meat, begin by cut your sausages whatever your brand of liking is. Cut them into slices making 6 - 10 pieces. Once they're done cutting, fry them, the fat of the sausages should help fry them quickly with no oil. But feel free to add some as well. If you're using ham or turkey, wait until after frying the eggs, as these meats tend to fry much quicker. Once they sausages are nice and crispy push them to the edge of the frying pan. Use the same grease and begin by frying your eggs, it makes it extra tasty. You can leave them sunny side up or fried, whatever you prefer.

Once they eggs are done, put them in a plate, add the meat to your side and begin warming up your tortillas. If you have a gas stove you can warm them up under the fire, if you have an electric stove, use the pan to warm up your tortillas. Once everything is done add the tapatio and enjoy! Delicious and easy breakfast!

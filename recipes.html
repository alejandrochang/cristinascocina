Recetas    |    recipes

Below find authentic Mexican recipes you have heard of but never created. From everyday meals to special meals for the holidays, you can find the recipes here!. There is nothing better than making food with family and enjoying each others company. Whether you're bringing food to your relatives, going to a potluck or want to try something new these recipes will sure diversify your cooking.

I will be adding recipes from time time. Feel free to send over an email if there is a specific recipe you're looking for. As for now go get cooking! 

Morisqueta

Rice, Beans, Sour Cream & More.

Morisqueta is the perfect meal-prep meal for the busy person who is trying to get a healthy start to their week. Providing the basics of nutrition and fiber for a snack or for dinner.

Flautas

How to make Chicken/Potato Flautas.

Flautas which look like what some people may know as 'taquitos' are fried tortillas wrapped traditionally with chicken, potato and chesse or both. They are relatively easy to make and delicious!

Huevos Estrellados

Huevos Estrellados.

Huevos Estrellados also known as fried eggs and Huevos Rancheros in restauraunts are a delicious classic Mexican Breakfast. Healthy and filling breakfast that can be done for the whole family.

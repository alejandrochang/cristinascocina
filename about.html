Hey There!

Welcome! My name is Alejandro, currently working as a Software Engineer in the San Francisco Bay Area documenting my families culinary history!

I have crafted this website to not only preserve my families history of culinary generations, but to share it with the world. Food is one of the few things in life that can tell you about history, family and congregation of people. What better way to share those values than through authentic Mexican recipes and recommendations!

Feel free to comment or send an email to say hello, give me insight as to whether you have enjoyed the recipes and on what more type of recipes you would like to see. Thanks again for visiting, now get cooking!
